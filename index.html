As you are aware, the COVID 19 pandemic has led to unprecedented and unanticipated challenges requiring collective action and support from all. While all necessary measures to fight the spread of Novel Corona Virus (COVID 19) are being effectively led by the Central Government and State Governments, there is a need to reinforce the importance of preventive measures and practices in a sustained manner, to deal with the disease over the long run.
Adopting COVID-19 appropriate behaviour is the best way to stop transmission of coronavirus, & its variants. From our past experience from several countries in the last one year, we have evidence that implementation of public health measures and adoption of COVID-19 appropriate behaviours are proven strategies to curtail virus transmission.

A SET OF 15 RULES, WHICH WE NEED TO FOLLOW AS A PART OF COVID APPROPRIATE BEHAVIOUR ARE AS SHOWN BELOW :--


How to use mask correctly :-

  • Always wear a mask or reusable face cover every time you move out of your residence.
  • Wear a mask properly so that your mouth & nose are fully covered
  • Change the mask if it becomes moist/wet
  • Don’t use a mask for more than 8 hours
FACTS :--

  • COVID-19, Corona Virus Disease, is a viral infectious disease. It is caused by recently discovered novel Coronavirus now named SARS-CoV-2.
  • The World Health Organization (WHO) declared COVID-19 a pandemic on 11 March 2020.
  • Most people (about 80%) recover from the disease without needing hospital treatment.
SELF MONITORING :--
  • If temperature >= 100.4 F (38º Celsius), then you have fever.
  • If your oxygen saturation i.e. SpO2 is below 95%, then immediately get your covid test done & contact the doctor.
